Motorcycle racing

Motorcycle racing is a sport where cyclists race on courses1. It is one of the most exciting sports in the world, but also one that requires the rider to be exceptionally alert and attentive2. The major categories of motorcycle racing are road racing, off road racing and track racing2. Road racing is the highest and most famous form of racing in the world under MotoGP.
